### Appendix C: Real Turtle Conservation Facts

For game accuracy and brand content:

- Females return to their birth beach to nest (20-30 years later)
- 1 in 1,000 hatchlings survives to adulthood
- They can live 50-80 years
- They migrate thousands of kilometers
- Main threats: fishing gear, plastic pollution, coastal development, poaching
- Dominican Republic hosts three nesting species
